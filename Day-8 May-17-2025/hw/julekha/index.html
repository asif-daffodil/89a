Lions are known for their power, social structure (prides), and distinctive roar, which contributes to their image as the dominant apex predator in the African savanna.

Tiger

Tigers are also considered powerful and dominant, especially in their Asian jungle habitats, and some argue for the tiger as the "real" king of the forest.

Crocodile

Crocodiles or true crocodiles are large, semiaquatic reptiles that live throughout the tropics in Africa, Asia, the Americas and Australia.
